Mind Dance - La danza de la mente
#Automaticas18 - Interactivas

Kinetic sculpture suspended with recycled speakers as a planetary system. The sounds and movements of these objects will be defined by muscular and brain sensors. The participant is invited to activate this sculpture with his own thoughts and movements reinterpreted by the designed system. The speakers reproduce sounds from the interior of the human body mixed with different music creations.
